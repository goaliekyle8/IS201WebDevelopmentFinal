Minnesota Minneapolis Mission

Hmong Speaking

From 2022-2024, I spent my time serving in the Minnesota Minneapolis Mission serving the Hmong people for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. I loved my time in Minnesota and made countless friends that I still keep in contact with today.

I served in Minneapolis and Saint Paul for the majority of my time in Minnesota. The cities are very diverse, and the Hmong are very prevalent. There are approximately 90,000 in Minnesota, and every one of them are very, very, kind.

Minnesota will always be a very special place to me, as it was where I came to know Jesus Christ and trust in him. There's a scripture I like that encapsulates this feeling:

And now it came to pass that all this was done in Mormon, yea, by the waters of Mormon, in the forest that was near the waters of Mormon; yea, the place of Mormon, the waters of Mormon, the forest of Mormon, how beautiful are they to the eyes of them who there came to the knowledge of their Redeemer; yea, and how blessed are they, for they shall sing to his praise forever. -Mosiah 18:30
